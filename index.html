FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything parents and students want to know about Sunrise Education and our Cambridge homeschool programme.

Is IGCSE homeschooling legal and recognised in Malaysia?
Yes! Cambridge IGCSE is fully recognised in Malaysia and by over 140 countries worldwide. Our students' certificates are accepted by universities in Malaysia, the UK, Australia, Singapore, the US, and beyond.
What is the age range for enrolment?
We welcome students from age 7 (Primary) to age 18 (Secondary). Students are placed in level-appropriate classes based on their individual ability and assessment — not just their age.
How is Sunrise different from a regular school?
Unlike mainstream schools, Sunrise offers personalised learning journeys, smaller class sizes, flexible scheduling, and an internationally accredited curriculum. Every child receives individual attention and progresses at their own optimal pace.
What pathways does IGCSE open up after graduation?
Cambridge IGCSE is the gateway to A-Level, Foundation Programmes, Pre-University Diplomas, and direct university entry. Our graduates have gone on to study at universities in Malaysia, the UK, Australia, and beyond.
How do I enrol my child at Sunrise?
Simply contact us via WhatsApp at +6 012-425 1556 or email LOVE@sun.edu.my to schedule a free consultation. We'll assess your child's level and recommend the best placement. Enrolment is open throughout the year!
What language is the instruction in?
Primary instruction is in English, following the Cambridge standard. We also offer Chinese Language classes and support Malay-speaking families. Our multilingual team is here to help every family feel at home.
